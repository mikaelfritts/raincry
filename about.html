Bringing Your Vision to Life Since 1995.

Welcome to Raincry, where we transform your ideas into captivating online experiences. We’re a small but mighty duo, working together seamlessly since 1995. With nearly three decades of expertise in website design and development, we’re passionate about helping businesses like yours stand out in a crowded digital world.

Who We Are

We’re Mikael and Matt — creatives, problem solvers, and lifelong collaborators. Over the years, we’ve partnered with clients of all sizes, from small businesses to growing enterprises, building websites that don’t just look great but deliver real results.

Our journey began in the early days of the internet (yes, we both had Geocities pages), and we’ve grown alongside the web, staying ahead of trends and technology to ensure our clients always receive cutting-edge solutions. We take pride in our ability to understand your unique needs and translate them into functional, engaging, and user-friendly websites that drive success.

What We Believe

At the heart of everything we do is our belief in creating meaningful connections through design and technology. We know your website is more than just an online presence—it’s your story, your brand, and your promise to your customers. That’s why we focus on:

  • Understanding Your Pain Points: Whether you’re struggling with an outdated site, poor functionality, or a lack of traffic, we’re here to listen and solve those challenges.
  • Personalized Solutions: Every business is unique, and we tailor our designs and development strategies to meet your goals. No cookie-cutter templates here—just custom work that reflects your brand’s personality.
  • Long-Term Success: We build websites that grow with your business, combining aesthetics with performance to help you thrive in the digital landscape.
